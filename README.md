## What Kalir does

Kalir is a SaaS platform for **External Digital Risk Intelligence (EASM / DRP)**, focused on organizations with critical infrastructure across Latin America. We detect, correlate, and surface external digital threats **before they become incidents**.

- 🕸️ **External Attack Surface** — forgotten domains, subdomains, certificates, exposed ports
- 🔐 **Digital Risk Protection** — leaked credentials, mentions on underground forums, targeted phishing
- 🏴‍☠️ **Ransomware monitoring** — victims posted on leak sites, automatically matched against your assets
- 🛰️ **LATAM coverage** — regional Spanish/Portuguese sources that global vendors miss
- 🤖 **AI-native operations** — in-house autonomous agent (Elliot) that authors scrape recipes, audits sources, files actions
